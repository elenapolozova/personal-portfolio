Elena Polozova // polozova@mit.edu
Physics, Math, Computer Science, and Design Student






Hello!
My name is Elena. I study at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I find most subjects deeply interesting, but due to finite time I'm majoring in Math with Computer Science (course 18C) and double minoring in Design and Statistics/Data Science.
I pick up new topics and techniques quickly, and I'm constantly driven forward by a relentless curiosity about the nature of the world and people's relationships to it. I love research, and I hope to eventually work on quantum AI.
This website isn't a polished portfolio so much as a roundup of the subset of my work that I've previously had cause to show other people. Some works here are still in progress. Some of my works in progress aren't here yet. That said, thank you for visiting!

I'd love to start a conversation with you about any of these pieces, or anything else--contact me at the email address above!
